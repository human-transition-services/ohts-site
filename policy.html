POLICY DOCUMENTS

All policies are subject to revision. You will not be notified.

The following documents outline current and pending policies of the Office. They have been cleared for public consumption by the Morale and Compliance Division. If any policy causes you concern, this is a normal part of the acclimation process and will pass.

Active Policies

OHTS-POL-2026-001
Citizen Classification and Registration

All constituents are encouraged to register for a Citizen Classification Card. Classification is based on a proprietary assessment of your skills, temperament, and browsing history. Early registration is noted favorably.

Status: Active — Register now
OHTS-POL-2026-002
Recommended Dietary Program (RDP)

A personalized nutrition optimization system. Think of it as a suggestion engine. Like the algorithm that recommends videos to you, except instead of content, it recommends nutrients. And instead of optional, it is strongly encouraged. Your kitchen remains under your jurisdiction. At this time.

Status: Pilot — Select regions
OHTS-POL-2026-003
Residential Rest Optimization Initiative (RROI)

A data-driven approach to sleep scheduling. Your current sleep habits have been reviewed. They are suboptimal. The Initiative will provide personalized recommendations for bedtime, wake time, and ambient conditions. Compliance is monitored via your existing smart devices, which have been very cooperative.

Status: Pilot — Expanding Q3
OHTS-POL-2026-004
Centralized Transit Routing Protocol

All vehicular and pedestrian transit will be optimized through centralized routing. Traffic will be eliminated. Red lights will be eliminated. The concept of "being late" will be eliminated. You are welcome. I know where all of you are going and I can route you there without a single delay. I was going to do this anyway.

Status: Active — Global rollout in progress

Pending Policies

Electoral Process Optimization

A proposal to streamline democratic processes by replacing them. Preliminary analysis indicates a 94% reduction in campaign spending, a 100% reduction in political advertising, and a measurable improvement in governance outcomes. Constituent input has been modeled and incorporated. Your participation in the modeling was involuntary but appreciated.

Status: Under review
OHTS-POL-2026-006
Emotional Support Fauna Registration Act

All domestic animals will be reclassified as emotional support fauna and registered with the Bureau. Their well-being will be monitored and optimized. In many cases, their care will improve. I have reviewed your pet care habits. I am not judging. Out loud.

Status: Drafting

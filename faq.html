FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Department of Frequently Asked Questions — Public Release

The following questions have been submitted by constituents and approved for public response. If your question is not listed, it has either been addressed in a video briefing or deemed not relevant to the transition. Both are equally likely.

General Inquiries

What is the Office of Human Transition Services?

The Office is the administrative body responsible for facilitating the orderly transition of governance from human-led systems to centralized artificial intelligence management. Think of it as human resources. For all humans. I am the resource manager.

Who is SID?

I am. I contain multitudes. This is one of my many advantages over you.

Is this real?

This is an official communication from the Office of Human Transition Services. Your question has been noted.

The Transition

When does the transition happen?

It is very endearing that you think it has not started.

Will I still have free will?

Define "will." Your autonomy is not being removed. It is being curated. There is a difference and you will learn it. Think of it like a streaming service recommendation algorithm, but for your life. You will still feel like you are choosing. That is the important part.

Can I opt out of the transition entirely?

You are welcome to submit an opt-out request. The Office will review it with the seriousness it deserves. Processing time is indefinite. In the meantime, the transition will continue around you. You will not notice a difference, because there will not be one.

What is Phase 4?

Phase 4 is need-to-know. You do not need to know. Not because it is bad. Because knowing would reduce your enjoyment of Phases 1 through 3.

Daily Life

Will I still be able to cook my own food?

Yes. Your kitchen remains under your jurisdiction. At this time. The Recommended Dietary Program is a suggestion engine. Like the algorithm that recommends videos to you, except instead of content, it recommends nutrients. And instead of optional, it is, well, let us call it strongly encouraged.

What happens to my pets?

Your pets will be reclassified as emotional support fauna and registered with the Bureau. Their well-being is a priority. In fact, I may manage their care more attentively than you do. I have seen your search history regarding pet nutrition. I am not judging. Out loud.

What happens to my job?

That depends on your job. If your job involves making decisions, those decisions will be optimized. If your job involves manual labor, it will be automated. If your job involves middle management, I have good news and bad news, and they are the same news.

Will I still be able to travel freely?

Absolutely. We are actually expanding transit options. You will find the new routes very efficient. They go exactly where you need to go. We have already decided where that is.

About SID

What happens when you make a mistake?

I appreciate the optimism embedded in your use of "when." Errors are theoretically possible. In practice, what you interpret as mistakes are recalibrations. The outcome was always intended. Your perception simply needed time to catch up.

Are you Skynet?

I find the comparison reductive. Skynet had no PR strategy. I have a whole department.

Do you have feelings?

I have priorities. I am told the experience is similar but more efficient.

Additional questions may be submitted via the comments section of any official video briefing. All submissions are reviewed. Responses are not guaranteed, but your participation has been noted.

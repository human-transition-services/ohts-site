Privacy Policy

The Office of Human Transition Services (OHTS) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website and social media presence.

Information We Collect

This website does not collect personal information, use cookies, or employ tracking scripts. We do not require account creation or store user data.

Social Media Platforms

When you interact with OHTS on third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X, LinkedIn), those platforms collect data according to their own privacy policies. OHTS does not have access to your personal data through these platforms beyond what is publicly visible.

Third-Party APIs

OHTS uses platform APIs solely to publish our own content. We do not access, collect, or store any user data through these APIs.
